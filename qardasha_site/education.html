Education

Education is an important part of Qardasha. The Islamic Sultanate relies on 3 main bodies for its education: Al-Mu’minoon Islamic Institute, the University of Emperor Orzan, and the Qardaishan House of Wisdom, a digital Islamic Library now merged with Al-Mu’minoon Islamic Institute.

The majority of the students of these educational bodies are online, however, that still doesn’t stop the education department from contributing greatly to the culture of the Islamic Sultanate and continuing to provide education for free.

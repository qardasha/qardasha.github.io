Diplomacy

The foreign policy of Qardasha is very open yet also somewhat strict. The Islamic Sultanate is open to diplomatic relations with any nation regardless of ideology or religion.

If you wish to set diplomatic relations with the Islamic Sultanate your nation must fit the following criteria:

  • Is at least 1 month old
  • Has a stable government
  • Has a citizen count of more than 5

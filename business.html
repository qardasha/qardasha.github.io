Business

Qardasha has a great range of businesses, most businesses sell a variety of digital commodities as well as real goods. Commodities range anywhere from works of art, profile pictures, PDFs, and other digital documents and software.

The Islamic Sultanate has many colors when it comes to the economy, the many businesses of Qardasha greatly contribute to the economy being the main source of spending for most citizens as well as the main source of revenue for some.

Munshi Publications

A state-owned service that not only publishes literary works of the people but also sells and distributes the aforementioned literary works.

The Bookmark

A private business that does translations, designs, and sells digital commodities that are related to literary works (i.e book covers and audios of poems), and explains literary works in meaning with the literary history.

Site Solutions

An international private-owned company based in Qardasha that makes websites, web applications, games, and more.

Royal Design Shop

A private-owned digital designing company that designs logos, and flags, make videos and creates as well as sells works of art.

Munshi Services

A private-owned agency that offers to write anything and everything for Qardaishans as well as translations.

Palma Dei

A foreign trading company that mainly sells digital works of art to Qardaishans (i.e comics, videos, art, etc.).

Pfp Store

A small state-owned business that provides profile pictures for people.
